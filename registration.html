Fee structure and payment details for Indian and International authors.

Registration Guidelines

At least one author of each accepted paper must complete registration and pay the appropriate fee for inclusion in the SEATNGA 2026 conference program and for submission to the publisher (subject to quality compliance and presentation at the conference).

  • Deadline: At least one author must register on or before 30 September 2025.
  • Eligibility for publication: Only registered and presented papers will be submitted to the publisher’s digital library (as applicable).
  • Each full registration covers one (1) paper up to 6 pages.
  • Up to 2 additional pages allowed with an overlength fee of ₹2500 / $30 per extra page.
  • Registration fees are non‑refundable.
  • Retain your payment transaction ID/receipt for the registration form.
  • IEEE/Student categories require a valid IEEE membership card or student ID upload (if applicable).
  • After payment, complete the Google Registration Form (one form per Paper ID) by 30 September 2025.
  • Late registration allowed up to 10 October 2025 with a 10% additional fee.
  • Select the correct category (Student/Professional, Virtual/Physical) and country (Indian/Foreign) while registering.
